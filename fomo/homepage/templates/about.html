About Us

The Family Oriented Music Organization is a family owned music store that’s been operating in Utah Valley since 1952. They have been profitable but are always looking for ways to improve their business.

“FOMO is a store that aims to satisfy the musical needs of musicians and budding musicians in the area. We provide instruments, accessories, repairs, sheet music, and training opportunities. Our store has four main revenue streams: sales, rentals, venue hosting and services.” -Johan Anderson

A Team of Experts

Our team includes members from a huge breadth of industries and musical backgrounds who have all gravitated toward the world of sound.

Our products include work from Fender, Yamaha, Casio, Gibson, Gretsch, Ibanez, PRS, Novation, Roland, Pure Tone and dozens of other globally-recognized compaines and brands

Our team members have taught, coached, and led lessons. We’ve organized, designed and facilitated professional learning and instruments.
